Existentia is a tool at the service of your quality of life. How do I contact my concierge ?

The simplest way: as soon as your account is activated, you will receive a telephone number to contact your concierge directly. We then create a group with your assigned concierges via the Whatsapp application, in which you can make all your requests to us.

What happens if my concierge cannot fulfil a specific request ?

Existentia is committed to implementing all the necessary means to respond to its members. In the event that a request cannot be met, your concierge will submit various proposals to suggest alternative solutions.

Are there any requests that Existentia is not able to provide ?

YES, the only limits are those relating to the different legislations.

Is the membership to Existentia individual ?

Does my membership have a minimum duration ?

YES, the subscription to Existentia is for a minimum period of one year.

What happens if I have a last-minute request ?

Existentia is committed to implementing all the necessary means to respond to its members. In the event that a request cannot be met, your concierge will submit various proposals to suggest alternative solutions.

Am I entitled to my own concierge ?

Your subscription gives you access to all the services and concierges of Existentia. Your requests will be treated as a priority by a designated concierge and in the event of unavailability, you will be put in contact with another concierge who will have instant access to your files. For certain complex files or at your express request, a single concierge can take charge of processing your entire request.

Can I cancel my membership ?

Yes, in accordance with the mozambican consumer code, you have fourteen (14) clear days, from the day after the confirmation of membership, to exercise your right of withdrawal by registered letter or email with acknowledgement of receipt. You also have the possibility to cancel your subscription before the annual renewal by indicating this to your concierge 1 month before the end of the anniversary date of your subscription. All you have to do is send us your cancellation request by registered letter.
For more information, please consult our General Terms and Conditions of Sale.

Can I gift a membership ?

YES. You can offer a subscription by sending an email to contact@existentiaservices.com or by phone.

What happens at the end of the year?

Existentia membership is tacitly renewable for one more year on each anniversary date of your subscription. To cancel your subscription after one year, simply send a registered letter one month before the anniversary date informing us of your wish to cancel.
